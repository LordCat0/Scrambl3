Scrambl3

Version 1.0

Makes scratch code a little harder to read by changing variable, list, sound, custom block, and sprite names.

⚠️NOTICE: This does NOT provide full security to your project, all this does is make it a little harder to read.

Made by Lord cat. Unpackager Protector is not affiliated with Scratch, Turbowarp, or PenguinMod. (This website was designed to look like Turbowarps unpackager.)

Supported Project Types

All scratch-like projects should work (Including .pmp)

Privacy

Files are processed locally on your computer and never sent to any server.
